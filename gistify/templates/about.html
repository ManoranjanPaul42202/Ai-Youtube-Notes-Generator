About Gistify

📚 Our Mission

Gistify transforms how you learn from YouTube videos. We believe that knowledge should be accessible and digestible, helping students, professionals, and lifelong learners save time and retain more information.

🚀 What We Do

Gistify uses cutting-edge AI technology to automatically transcribe YouTube videos and generate smart, structured notes. Whether you're watching educational content, tutorials, or lectures, we turn hours of video into concise, actionable notes.

✨ Key Features

  • ✅ Automatic Transcription: Get accurate transcripts from any YouTube video
  • ✅ AI-Powered Summaries: Intelligent note generation with different styles
  • ✅ Timestamp Tracking: Jump to specific moments in videos
  • ✅ Save & Export: Keep your notes organized and export anytime
  • ✅ Secure & Private: Your data stays safe and confidential
